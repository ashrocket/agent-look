Tired of dragging Screenshot-2026-03-22-at-2.15.32-PM.png into the Claude command line and then having to explain what was wrong?

Same. I am so lazy.

Well now you don't have to drag those files — or add them to Claude Desktop either.

Set your screenshot directory once. Take your screenshots. Come back to Claude and say /look (or just "look at these screenshots from the app"). Claude finds what you captured, shows you a preview list, and only loads what you pick.

It even renames the generic filenames based on what's actually in the image.
